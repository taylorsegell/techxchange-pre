### About us

We are experienced content and skills specialists for the **Data and AI**
solutions that are part of the worldwide **IBM Technology Sales** organization.
Our charter is to create compelling and quality content and provide easy access
to reliable and trusted sales demos and environments to enable, and sharpen the
skills of IBM sellers and business partners on how to effectively present,
demonstrate, and sell IBM's data fabric solution, **Cloud Pak for Data**.
